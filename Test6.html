Тест по теме «Напряженный»

Choose the right tense in each sentence.

1) I saw the picture James _____ of you. It's amazing!

draw
drawn
drew

2) I'm hungry, let's ____ !

eat
eaten
ate

3) You have to _____ the bottle before you open it.

shook
shake
shaken

4) I had my picture ____ by a professional photographer.

taken
took
take

5) What time do you usually _____ up.

wake
woke
woken

6) Ivan _____ off the horse and broke his arm.

fall
fallen
fell

7) The presentation ____ at 8pm but we were 10 minutes late.

begun
began
begin

8) Have you ____ your pen again? Here, borrow mine.

forgot
forget
forgotten

9) She _____ me an umbrella when it was raining.

given
give
gave

10) I haven't _____ my homework yet.

done
did
do
